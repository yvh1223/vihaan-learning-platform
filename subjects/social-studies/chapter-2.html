Geography & Interactive Maps

Explore Texas geography, U.S. regions, and world maps through interactive activities and virtual exploration!

Unit Overview

Practice map skills, explore Texas and U.S. regions, and connect physical geography to culture and economy.

Big Ideas

  • Physical features (mountains, rivers, plains) shape how people live and work.
  • Regions share common landforms, climates, and resources.
  • Maps use tools like scale, compass rose, and legends to share information.
  • Human movement changes communities and the environment.

Skills You Will Practice

  • Read maps using a legend, scale, and cardinal directions.
  • Compare regions by climate, resources, and landforms.
  • Explain how people adapt to different environments.
  • Use geographic vocabulary to describe places.

Essential Questions

  • How do landforms and climate influence daily life?
  • What makes a region different from its neighbors?
  • How do maps help us make decisions?
