Social Studies

Explore American history, geography, government, and economics through interactive timelines, maps, and civic simulations.

What You Will Learn

Build strong social studies skills by analyzing evidence, reading maps, and connecting past events to today.

History

  • Place events in order using timelines.
  • Compare perspectives from different groups.
  • Identify causes and effects of change.

Geography

  • Read maps with legends, scale, and direction.
  • Explain how landforms and climate shape life.
  • Explore regions of Texas and the U.S.

Civics & Economics

  • Understand how government protects rights.
  • Describe goods, services, and trade-offs.
  • Connect culture and community traditions.
