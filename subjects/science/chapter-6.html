üåø Conservation Lab

Conservation means using resources wisely so they last longer. Small choices make a big difference.

  • Water: Fix leaks, take shorter showers, and turn off the tap while brushing.
  • Energy: Switch off lights, unplug chargers, and use sunlight when possible.
  • Waste: Reduce, reuse, and recycle to keep trash out of landfills.

üìù Key Terms

  • Renewable resource: A resource that can be replaced naturally in a short time.
  • Nonrenewable resource: A resource that is limited and forms very slowly.
  • Conservation: Using resources wisely to prevent waste.
  • Pollution: Harmful substances added to air, water, or land.
  • Sustainability: Meeting needs today while protecting resources for the future.
