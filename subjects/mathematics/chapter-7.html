üìò Money Basics

Financial literacy starts with understanding key words and habits.

Important Words

  • Income: money you earn.
  • Expense: money you spend.
  • Savings: money you keep.
  • Budget: a plan for your money.

Fixed vs. Variable

  • Fixed: same cost each time.
  • Variable: changes week to week.

Example: bus pass (fixed) and snacks (variable).

Money Habit

Try the 50/30/20 idea:

  • 50% needs
  • 30% wants
  • 20% savings

Needs vs. Wants

  • Needs: food, school supplies, transportation.
  • Wants: toys, games, extra snacks.

Smart shoppers compare prices and think about needs first.
